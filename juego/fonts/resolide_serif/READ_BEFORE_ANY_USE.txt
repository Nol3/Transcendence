Resolide Serif is a confident display typeface with high contrast and a sculptural feel. Its pronounced wedge serifs and deep vertical cuts give it a modern sharpness while nodding to classic Roman letterforms. The rhythm of its forms dances between shadow and light, creating a dramatic interplay especially in large sizes.

The lowercase is designed with elegance and precision, complementing the bold geometry of the capitals. Whether used in branding or on a book cover, Resolide Serif stands with poise and distinction.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Designed and created by Mans Greback, a type designer known for his craftsmanship in developing professional and creative fonts for both expressive and formal typography.
